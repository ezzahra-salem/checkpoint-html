The matrix 1999

Matrix is ​​a science fiction film directed by the brothers Andy and Larry Wachowski and released in 1999. Inspired among other things by the episode "Dreams for sale" from the series Twilight Zone (1985) and probably by anticipation books, such as Isaac Asimov's robots or the Neuromancer, it is the first part of a trilogy which will continue with the films Matrix Reloaded and Matrix Revolutions.
